Pinki Pradhan

PhD Student at NISER Bhubaneswar

Google Scholar | DBLP | ORCID

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Theoretical Computer Science at NISER Bhubaneswar, under the supervision of Dr. Anup Bhattacharya. My research broadly focuses on the design and analysis of algorithms, with particular emphasis on Randomized Algorithms, Sublinear Algorithms and Beyond-worst case analysis of Algorithms. I also enjoy thinking about Clustering problems under limited or noisy information, including adversarial settings, and the beyond worst-case analysis of clustering problems.
