About Me

I am a PhD student in Theoretical Computer Science at NISER Bhubaneswar, working under the supervision of Dr. Anup Bhattacharya. My research focuses on the design and analysis of algorithms, particularly Randomized Algorithms and Sublinear Algorithms. I am also interested in clustering problems under limited or noisy information, including adversarial settings.
